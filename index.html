What is SIP Calculator?

A SIP Calculator is a free online tool that helps you calculate the returns on your Systematic Investment Plan (SIP). Enter your monthly investment amount, expected return rate, and investment period to instantly see your potential wealth growth and total investment returns.

How to calculate Home Loan EMI?

To calculate your home loan EMI:

  1. Enter your loan amount
  2. Select your interest rate
  3. Choose loan tenure in years
  4. Get instant EMI amount, total interest payable, and amortization schedule

Our calculator also shows you a detailed breakup of principal and interest components.

How is monthly SIP return calculated?

Monthly SIP returns are calculated using the compound interest formula for regular monthly investments. Our calculator considers:

  • Monthly investment amount
  • Expected annual return rate
  • Investment duration
  • Power of compounding

The calculator shows both your total investment and the wealth gained through compounding.
